== Description ==
With the lead generator for free real estate valuation you can easily and professionally create landing pages in just a few minutes. With this you are sustainably competitive in online marketing. We are constantly improving the Lead Generator to provide the best user experience possible. This will convert prospective customers into leads even more effectively. Try it now for free.

Your advantages with this Lead-Generator:

* User friendly
* Device optimized
* Freely customizable
* Simple and fast integration
* Continuous improvements

== Installation ==
So that your personal lead generator is ready for use, all you need after the installation and activation of the plugin is to store your individual access authorization (API token) under Settings > Real Estate Leads in WordPress.
You can find your API token after complete registration on [LeadMarkt.ch](https://leadmarkt.ch/#loslegen) in the settings.
